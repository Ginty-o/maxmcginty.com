Finite Element Analysis

I have had some experince with finite element analysis in other projects. In all of the FIRST robots and in the heavy lift drone I have used Autodesk Inventor's stress anlysis tool to confirm design choices and material selection. I have also began learning how to do computational flow design and heat transfer in Autodesk CFD. Through that I began to lean about the mechanics of CFD however I still have a surface level understanding.

This picture is showing expected stress on the frame of my Heavy lift Drone due to torque and shows how the frame may deform due to other forces. This shows how I can improve my design by looking at stress points in my frame.

Here I attempt to validate the mounting for my drone's motors. by using a expected forces from thrust data from the manufacture I can see if my mounting solution will fail or deform to an extent that would effect performance
